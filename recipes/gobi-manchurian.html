Indian Recipes: Gobi Manchurian

About the dish

Gobi Manchurian is sub class of an indo-chinese dish called Manchurian.

Manchurian is made by chopping and deep-frying an ingredient - e.g. chicken, prawns paneer, or cauliflower (gobi) and is a staple of Indian Chinese cuisine.

Hence its name, Gobi Manchurian is the version of Manchurian made with cauliflower.

Things you will need

  • 15 cauliflower florets
  • oil
  • 5 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 4 tablespoons of cornmeal
  • 70 ml of water
  • a teaspoon of ground black peppers
  • salt
  • 180 ml of vegetable stock
  • a tablespoon of ketchup
  • a tablespoon of soy sauce
  • a tablespoon of chile-garlic sauce
  • half of a onion (sliced)
  • a finely chopped green chile pepper
  • 3 cloves of miced garlic
  • a teaspoon of finely chopped ginger

Recipe

    First we will prepare the gobi or cauliflower...

  1. Prepare for deep-frying by boiling the oil in a pan (preferrably a frying pan)
  2. Mix the 5 tablespoons of flour, 3 tablespoons of cornmeal, 70 ml of water,1 teaspoon of black pepper, and 3/4 of a tablespoon of salt in a bowl such that it becomes like batter
  3. Put in the cauliflower florets
  4. Mix the cauliflower with the batter-like mixture until it coats each floret
  5. Add 2-4 newly coated cauliflower florets into the pan prepared for deep-frying
  6. Fry for 3 minutes
  7. Move the fried cauliflowers from the pan into a bowl with paper at the bottom for absorbing the oil
  8. Repeat steps 5 to 7 until all the florets are done frying

    9. Now we will prepare the sauce...

  9. Mix 60 ml of vegetable stock with the remaining tablespoon of cornmeal
  10. Add a tablespoon of soy sauce, a tablespoon of ketchup, a tablespoon of chile-garlic sauce, and a adequate amount of salt

    11. Finally, to combine it all...

  11. Clean and reheat the pan with just a tablespoon of oil
  12. Add the sliced onions, green chile pepper, garlc, and ginger and stir-fry for a few seconds
  13. Add the recently prepared sauce and cook for 2 minutes
  14. Add remaining vegetable stock and cook until the mixture is very thick
  15. Add the previously prepared cauliflowers into the sauce and toss untill it is well covered and mixed

    16. ... And your gobi manchurian is ready to serve
